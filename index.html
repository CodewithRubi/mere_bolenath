ADIYOGI

Adiyogi, often referred to as the "first yogi," is a significant figure in Hindu mythology and spirituality. According to legend, Adiyogi is considered the progenitor of yoga, the ancient science of self-realization and union with the divine. The word "Adiyogi" itself translates to "the first yogi" or "the primal yogi" in Sanskrit.

ॐ त्र्यम्बकं यजामहे सुगन्धिं पुष्टिवर्धनम् उर्वारुकमिव बन्धनान् मृत्योर्मुक्षीय मामृतात्
